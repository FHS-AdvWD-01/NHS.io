NHS Application Process

Maintain a necessary cumulative minimum grade point average of 3.75. At the beginning of junior year, qualifying applicants will receive a invitation letter to apply for NHS. Applicants will attend a mandatory information session outlining the remainder of the application process.

How to Ensure a Strong Application

Students are encouraged to complete community service hours during their freshmen and sophomore years. All community service hours completed from the summer before the applicant's freshmen year will count toward their NHS application. Always be sure to follow the policies and guidelines for students as printed in the Student/Parent Handbook as demonstration of character. Be sure to get involved in clubs/activities/outside organizations to demonstrate leadership abilities.
