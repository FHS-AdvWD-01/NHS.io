NHS applicant requirements

Maintain the grade point average necessary for acceptance as a demonstration of Scholarship. At the beginning of the academic year, each memberâ€™s GPA will be checked. Follow the policies and guidelines for students as printed in the Student/Parent Handbook as demonstration of Character. Maintain an acceptable level of participation in NHS activities as stated in the NHS Activities Requirements as demonstration of Service and Leadership.

Maintain a necessary cumulative minimum grade point average of 3.75. At the beginning of junior year, qualifying applicants will receive a invitation letter to apply. Applicants will attend a mandatory information session outlining the remainder of the application process. To ensure a strong application, students are encouraged to complete community service hours during their freshmen and sophomore years. All community service hours completed from the summer before the applicant's freshmen year will count toward their NHS application.
